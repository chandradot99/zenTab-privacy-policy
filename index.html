ZenTab - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

ZenTab is a Chrome extension that transforms your new tab into a productivity dashboard. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

ZenTab does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All information you enter (tasks, notes, important dates, preferences) is stored locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in storage system.

Permissions Explained

  • Storage Permission: Used to save your tasks, notes, important dates, and preferences locally on your device. This data never leaves your computer.
  • Host Permissions (if applicable): Used only for optional features like weather information or background images. No personal data is transmitted with these requests.

Local Data Storage

All your data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API, which means:

  • Your tasks, notes, and dates stay on your device
  • No data is uploaded to our servers or any third-party services
  • You can export your data at any time
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data

Third-Party Services

ZenTab may optionally use external services for enhanced functionality:

  • Weather APIs for displaying weather information (location-based requests only, no personal data stored)
  • Image services for background photos (no personal data involved)

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any user data to third parties. All data remains local to your device.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security. We recommend keeping your browser and operating system updated.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export your data using the built-in export feature
  • Delete specific items or clear all data
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all stored data

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify users through the extension or update the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or ZenTab, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page.

This privacy policy is effective as of the last updated date above.
